If you select an invoice from the dropdown list a few things will happen when you click "Save and Invoiced":
- If the Project Cost Centre is setup to be Cybersource, but the client is NOT Cybersource the invoice will get split the way invoices usually do with the addition of a couple of extra transactions will appear on the invoice (within alloc: perhaps a Payment Insurance Transaction(s) or commission transactions etc), and you will be taken to the actual invoice to view the additions.

- If *no* invoice is selected and the client & cost centre are Cybersource, transactions will created and marked approved immediately.
