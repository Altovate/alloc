New Task Address

Emails sent to this email address by alloc users will automatically get turned into new tasks.

The subject line of the email becomes the task's name. The body of the email becomes the task's long description. Any attachments on the email will be saved as attachments to the task.

Ensure the address you enter here is different to the "From Address" that you specify above.

However, when emails are sent to the email address you enter for the "New Task Address", the emails should get forwarded to the "From Address".
