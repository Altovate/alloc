Quick List

The Quick List dropdown contains actions that are used frequently. It also stores a bit of your allocPSA browsing history. So if you were just editing a Task and you've forgotten what it was called, take a look in the Quick List and it might be there.

Search

The Search allows you to quickly search through the main entities in allocPSA. If your search text is a number, ie an entity ID, then the search will redirect you straight to that entity.
