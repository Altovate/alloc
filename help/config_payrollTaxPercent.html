Payroll Tax Percent

In Australia if work is done through an agency then the employee does not need to pay a special tax called Payroll Tax (which is normally deducted by the employing company without the employee being aware). Within allocPSA if you tick the checkbox of a Project marked "Through an Agency (Payroll Tax Exempt)" then the percentage that the organization takes is lessened by the percentage specified here.

So for example if the Company Percent field below is set to 30% and the Payroll Tax is set to 5%. Then provided the Project has its "Through an Agency" box checked, the amount that the Company will deduct will be 25% instead of 30%.
