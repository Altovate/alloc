Project Summary Filter

This filter will allow you to filter the Tasks that are graphed for a Project by Task Type and Task Status.

Multiple Task Types may be selected by holding Control or the Shift key whilst clicking the items with your mouse.

Note, in some web browsers after you click the Filter button, you may have to do a hard refresh to avoid seeing the previously cached graph image. You can usually do this with an Ctrl-R, or Ctrl-Shift-R, or holding Control whilst hitting the Refresh button with your mouse.
